tales from the shadownet

Tales from the Shadownet is (will be) a webseries about net neutrality. My partners and I have five half-hour episodes planned; the first episode is fully written and in pre-production now.

In the series, private underground networks operate in secret, hiding from the watchful eyes of both the law and huge corporations. Sometimes they're heavily-protected local area networks in speakeasy - style internet cafes; sometimes it's simply hard drives loaded up with the latest censured data and physically ferried to users. But it always happens in the shadows.

batgirls united

Batgirls United is a planned fanseries of ten 5-6 minute episodes about the DC characters who have donned the mantle of "Batgirl."

It picks up when Stephanie Brown is assigned to help a newly-paralyzed Barbara Gordon with her recovery following the events of The Killing Joke storyline, and follows them and former assassin Cassandra Cain as together they all find a place in Gotham’s crime - fighting landscape.

murder mystery party kits

I also write kits for "murder mystery" party games— events between 2-5 hours, usually, where guests are given a character to play. Each character has their own goals and motivations, and it's up to the players to try to get those accomplished as well as solve a larger crime or other plotline. (Usually, this is the murder of an uncast character.)
